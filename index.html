Our Story

Born in Bengal,
Worn by the World

Ruma's Sajghor is a Bengali-inspired fashion destination where every outfit reflects individuality, tradition, and modern elegance. We believe fashion is more than clothing — it's a way of expressing your unique story.

Our collections are lovingly curated from artisan weavers, block printers, and designers who carry centuries of craft in their hands. We bring their work to you — with a modern sensibility that honours the past while embracing today.
